ABOUT THE SERVICE

What is Erenta?

Erenta is trusted marketplace for renting VideoGames, Sports and adventure equipments, Accessories and Electronic items. We connect people who need things with the people who have them So it is an online peer to peer rental platform.

Why use Erenta as borrower?

When you choose our service over buying from stores you get the following benefits -:

  1. Avoid unnecessry buying items which you use few times, Accept renting it takes fraction of cost and save money.
  2. Live lighter, Save the planet by reducing waste. By opting to borrow, not buy, you’re putting the brakes on unnecessary manufacturing and carbon-intensive distribution systems. It is the responsible choice.
  3. Quality and different varieties of products.
  4. Catch up with your passion,hobby and get things done. You can rent things in affordable prices, Whether you want to do photography or play with playstation or going on hiking trip or going to play or learn new outdoor sports these are few screnrios where our service comes in the role.

How renting works?

  1. Find What you are looking for, from category section or by search bar.
  2. Book the item for your chosen dates and let us verify you. we will just once ask some documents for proof.
  3. Arrange collection from your lender and enjoy your rental.
  4. Return the item to the lender and leave the review on our platform. we are always happy to hear your experience.

Documents required?

Every user submits ID card as part of our Smart Verification process, so that you can rent in confidence.
